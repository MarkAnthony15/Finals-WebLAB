Frequently Asked Questions

Not sure where to start and how to proceed? Take a look at the questions commonly asked to us or contact us if you have other questions.



Are there any restrictions to travel?


Generally, there are no restrictions as to where you can travel as long as you bring the vehicle back on time and in the same condition it was given to you.



What are the qualifications a driver must have?


Aside from the ability to professionally drive, the driver must present a current and accepted professional license.



What documentation is required when picking up a car?


You must present your full valid professional driving license and a credit card in your name.



What methods of payment do you accept? When do I pay my rental?


We only accept cash. Down payment must be given upon pick-up and the rest, along with additional payment if there are any, will be given upon return of the vehicle.



What happens if I fail to return the vehicle on the agreed time?


Additional payment shall be charged, depending on the hours of delay.



Are there any mileagae restrictions?


No, we offer unlimited mileage so you can drive as far as you like.



Can I upgrade a car, once I have made a booking?


Usually, it is not a problem to upgrade a vehicle, as long as the replacement car is available. We will do all that we can to accomodate any changes you wish to make to your rental.



Can I rent a vehicle one way?


All our rental bookings are for pick-up and drop-off.



What does the insurance include?


All CarGet vehicles are fully covered by insurance.



Do you provide any optional products?


GPS is availabe.



What happens if the car breaks down?


In the unlikely even of a mechanical breakdown, contact us so that we may assist you in getting help.



What happens if I have to cancel my rental?


All cancellations must be done 24 hours before the booked pick-up and are subject to a cacellation fee equivalent to 20% of the original booking.



Will my rental car be an automatic or manual transmission?


The majority of our vehicles have automatic transmissions, however, you can request and specify a manual transmission vehicle that you prefer during your booking.
